Medgar Tully was the Lord of Riverrun and head of House Tully during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.[4]

History

Lord Medgar participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow. He challenged Ser Humfrey Hardyng in his first tilt and was knocked from the saddle on the second lance, was defeated on foot with swords.[1] He died within two years after the Tourney, because in 211 AC the unnamed Lord Tully of Riverrun is a boy of eight.[3]
