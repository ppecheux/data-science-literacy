Beron Stark was Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen.[1][4] Beron became Lord of Winterfell after his brother Lord Rodwell Stark died without issue. He is the second son of Lord Brandon Stark and Lady Alys Karstark.[3]

History

Lord Beron gathered swords to throw Lord Dagon Greyjoy's ironmen back into the sea and made common cause with the Lord of Casterly Rock for it.[5][4] He was mortally wounded in the ensuing clash, and House Stark faced a crisis of succession as his wife and four other recent Stark widows made claim for their own children.[6][7]

He is buried in the crypts of Winterfell.[1]

Family

Lord Beron was married to Lady Lorra Royce, with whom he had seven children; Donnor, Willam, Artos, Berena, Alysanne, Errold and Rodrik Stark.[3]
