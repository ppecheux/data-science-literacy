Marghaz zo Loraq is a Meereen noble, and is a cousin to Hizdahr zo Loraq.[1]

Appearance

Marghaz is overweight, with heavy legs, and tries to hide his physique behind a muscled breastplate. He also wears a lion's mask.[2]

Recent Events

A Dance with Dragons

He is made the new commander of the Brazen Beasts by Hizdahr zo Loraq after he marries queen Daenerys Targaryen, replacing Skahaz mo Kandaq.[1]

Barristan Selmy describes Marghaz as fawning to superiors, harsh to inferiors and as blind as he was boastful and too proud by half, comparisons are made between Marghaz and Janos Slynt.[2]
