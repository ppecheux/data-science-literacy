Lord Brandon Stark was the Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.[4][3] He was the youngest son of Lord Cregan Stark and Lady Lynara Stark.[3]

History

Brandon succeeded his brother Barthogan Stark as Lord of Winterfell at some time during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.[4] He was married to Lady Alys Karstark, with whom he had three children: Rodwell, Beron and Arsa Stark. He also had a bastard son, Lonnel Snow, with Lady Wylla Fenn.[3]

He is buried in the crypts of Winterfell.[1]
