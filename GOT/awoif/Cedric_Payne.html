Cedric Payne
Title Ser
Allegiance House Payne
Culture Westermen
Died In 298 AC, at Riverlands
Book(s) A Feast for Crows (mentioned)

Ser Cedric Payne was a knight of House Payne.

History

Ser Cedric took Podrick Payne in after he was abandoned by his mother. Podrick squired for Ser Cedric until the War of the Five Kings where during Lord Tywin Lannister's campaign in the Riverlands, Ser Cedric died.[1]
