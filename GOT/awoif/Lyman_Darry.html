Lyman Darry
Title Lord of Darry
Allegiance House Darry
Culture Rivermen
Born In 290 AC or 291 AC[1]
Died In 299 AC, at Darry
Book(s) A Game of Thrones (appears)
A Clash of Kings (mentioned)

Lyman Darry is a member of House Darry and the son and heir of Ser Raymun Darry.

Recent Events

A Game of Thrones

Lyman is brought to Riverrun by Ser Karyl Vance and Ser Marq Piper after the Lannister siege has been lifted.[2]

A Clash of Kings

Castle Darry is retaken by the forces of House Darry, but they hold it less than a fortnight until Ser Gregor Clegane comes and puts the entire castle to the sword, including Lyman.[3]

Quotes

Marq: Whatever you may decide for yourselves, I shall never call a Lannister my king.
Lyman: Nor I! I never will![2]

—Marq Piper and Lyman at Riverrun
