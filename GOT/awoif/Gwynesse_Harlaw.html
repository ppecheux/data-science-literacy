Lady Gwynesse Harlaw is the sister of Lord Rodrik Harlaw. She is seven years older.[1]

History

Gwynesse's husband died during the sea battle off Fair Isle during Greyjoy's Rebellion in 289 AC.[1] Following his death, Gwynesse returned to Ten Towers, where she has remained since. One of the towers of the castle was renamed the Widow's Tower in her honor. She believes that the castle should be hers since she is older than Lord Rodrik Harlaw, and constantly reminds her brother of this.[1]

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

Lord Rodrik Harlaw tells his niece, Asha Greyjoy, that his sister Gwynesse continues to mourn her husband as deeply as she did when he died. She has grown forgetful, however, and cannot always recall his name.[1]

A Dance with Dragons

After fleeing the kingsmoot at Old Wyk, Asha briefly visits Gwynesse and her mother, Alannys Harlaw, at Ten Towers before sailing for Deepwood Motte.[2]

Quotes

I am seven years his elder. Ten Towers should be mine.[2]

—Gwynesse to Asha Greyjoy
